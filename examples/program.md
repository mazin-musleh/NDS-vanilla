<section id="aboutProgram" class="nds-content-section">
    <div class="nds-section-wrapper">
        <div class="nds-section-head">
            <h2 class="nds-section-title">About the Program</h2>
        </div>
        <div class="nds-section-body nds-expandable">
            <div class="nds-block nds-expandable-content" style="--max-height:300px">
                <p>The Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence program equips students with a strong foundation in computer science, mathematics, and the core disciplines of AI including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. The program emphasizes both theoretical understanding and practical application, preparing graduates for careers in industry, research, and entrepreneurship.</p>
                <p>Students engage in hands-on projects, internships, and a capstone research experience. The curriculum is designed in alignment with international standards and industry needs, ensuring graduates are prepared to contribute to Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 digital transformation goals and the global AI ecosystem.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>

                    <ol>
                        <li>Provide students with a solid foundation in mathematics, statistics, and computer science necessary for AI research and development.</li>
                        <li>Develop expertise in core AI disciplines including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.</li>
                        <li>Foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and research skills to address real-world challenges using AI techniques.</li>
                        <li>Prepare graduates to design, implement, and evaluate intelligent systems that are ethical, fair, and transparent.</li>
                        <li>Cultivate collaboration and communication skills for effective teamwork in multidisciplinary environments.</li>
                        <li>Support Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 by producing AI professionals capable of driving digital transformation across industries.</li>
                    </ol>

                    <ul>
                        <li>Apply mathematical and computational foundations to model and solve AI problems.</li>
                        <li>Design and implement machine learning pipelines for structured and unstructured data.</li>
                        <li>Build and train deep neural networks for tasks in vision, language, and reinforcement learning.</li>
                        <li>Evaluate AI models using appropriate metrics, statistical tests, and ablation studies.</li>
                        <li>Analyze ethical implications of AI systems and apply principles of responsible AI.</li>
                        <li>Communicate technical findings effectively through written reports and oral presentations.</li>
                        <li>Collaborate in team settings to deliver end-to-end AI projects from problem definition to deployment.</li>
                    </ul>
