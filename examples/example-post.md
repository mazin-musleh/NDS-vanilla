                <h2>Launch Ceremony</h2>
                <p>
                    In a celebration of innovation and digital excellence, the Digital Government Authority officially
                    launched the National Design System, marking a significant milestone in the Kingdom's digital
                    transformation journey.
                </p>
                <p>
                    The event brought together government officials, digital designers, developers, and stakeholders
                    from across the Kingdom to witness the unveiling of this comprehensive design framework that will
                    standardize digital experiences across all government platforms.
                </p>

                <h2>By The Numbers</h2>
                <p>
                    The National Design System represents months of research, design, and development:
                </p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>50+ Components</strong> - Reusable UI components ready for implementation</li>
                    <li><strong>200+ Design Tokens</strong> - Centralized design decisions for colors, typography, and
                        spacing</li>
                    <li><strong>100+ Documentation Pages</strong> - Comprehensive guides and examples</li>
                </ul>

                <h2>Opening Remarks</h2>
                <blockquote

                    "The National Design System represents our commitment to providing citizens with seamless,
                    accessible, and consistent digital experiences across all government touchpoints. This is a

                <h2>Key Features</h2>
                <p>
                    The design system includes comprehensive tools and resources for government agencies:
                </p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>Component Library</strong> - Pre-built, accessible UI components ready for
                        implementation</li>
                    <li><strong>Design Tokens</strong> - Centralized design decisions for colors, typography, and
                        spacing</li>
                    <li><strong>Accessibility Guidelines</strong> - WCAG 2.1 AA compliance built into every component
                    </li>
                    <li><strong>Multi-language Support</strong> - Full RTL and LTR support for Arabic and English</li>
                    <li><strong>Responsive Design</strong> - Mobile-first approach ensuring optimal experiences on all
                        devices</li>
                    <li><strong>Interactive Documentation</strong> - Live code examples and comprehensive usage
                        guidelines</li>
                </ul>
